Dating Site Reviews

Cycling Passions

Cycling Passions is perhaps one of my favorite dating sites and that is because it links two markets into one social site - Cycling and Dating.

It was a little confusing, as its header includes a lot of advertisements, I click on Browse Singles Now and ended up at a Filipinos Singles Site. After hitting the back button, I realized that I had clicked on an Ad. The site advertises to be free, which is likely true as there are ads everywhere to the point that it makes navigation somewhat confusing.

In creating an account, I ended up on an entirely different dating site, Looxy.com, which looked pretty suspect to me. To actually start chatting with folks, I'd need a Paid Subscription. Thus draw me in and get my money. Pretty much the same old story of most dating site.

Although I would not recommend Cycling Passions I'm including the link for completeness.

Looxy

Since I was tricked into joining the site, I figured why not review it. The f irst thing to point out is that the site is filled with links to other pages or scripts to the point I'm essentially scared for the safety of my computer. But hey as a determined journalist, I must venture on.

The site starts out with a typical carousel of photos that you can like or skip. The top banner shows that a couple women have messaged me, which I find surprising since I've only been on the site for a few minutes, but nonetheless, I take the bait and click there. I find that a couple of women have messaged me and I have the option to submit a cureated message for a list box back to them. As I click on other links like Visitors, Likes You & Matches, I'm prompted to buy a subscription.

All in all the site looks like a total scan and has a low trust rating from Scam Advisors. Thus stay far far away.

Date Hookup

Date Hookup advertises itself as a totally free dating site. After giving it very little demographic information, it shows a grid of profiles. After clicking on a profile and attempting to communicate using meet me free option. This took me to a usename, password, email form and after completing that I was provided numerous pages asking all kinds of Dating Profile questions. As I look up, I see that I am actually on Match.Com!

Seems Date Hookup just sends you over to Match.com.

Not my cup of tea but why not take a look at Date Hookup
