Scrapbooking Site Reviews

DaisyTrail Digital Scrapbooking

DaisyTrail is solely focused on Digital Scrap Booking. The have numerous user groups and allow registered users to create their own groups. Groups are typically interest based, such as groups of members that are using the same scrap booking software packages. This organization allows members to help other members, share their works and get great digital scrap booking ideas.

Please check them out DasiyTrail Digital Scrapbooking

ScrapbookFlair

ScrapbookFlair supports a global community of scrap booking enthusiast. They have a very rich offering including Galleries, Groups, Challenges, Blogs, Forums, a Market Place and much more. The forums are very active and include topics around children, games, challenges & contests as well as discussions on software and starting a scrap booking business.

Please check them out ScrapbookFlair

Scrapbook Retreat Directory

While not technically a Social Networking Site for Scrap Bookers, Scrapbook Retreat Directory is worth listing here as it provides a right list of places where Scrap Bookers can get away individually or in groups and, well, scrapbook.

Please check them out Scrapbook Retreats
